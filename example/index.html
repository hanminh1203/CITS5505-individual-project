Key Terms

Glossary

Term
Context Window

The maximum amount of text an LLM can read and reason over in a single interaction.

Term
Temperature

Controls randomness in AI output. Lower = more deterministic; higher = more creative.

Term
Embeddings

Numerical representations of text that allow AI to understand semantic similarity.

Term
Fine-tuning

Further training a pre-trained model on a specific dataset to specialise its behaviour.
